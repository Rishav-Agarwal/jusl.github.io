About Us

This is an educational website currently under development. It is being developed by the students of Jadavpur Uiversity. It aims at providing course contents to the students of Jadavpur University only. The objective is to host materials on all courses taught in Jadavpur University, make course related materials easily and reliably available and act as a guide to the learning path for a particular course. Basically we want to help students to have all resources easily available for better learning of a particular course. Presently, we are focusing only on Department of Information Technology. In future, however, we would expand to include other departments.

How can you benefit from this website?

We intend to provide content like lecture slides, class notes, assignments, previous year question papers and solved solutions for majority of the courses.

We also plan to create study guides of various courses. We plan to accumulate all the videos sources and links which might be helpful for better understanding of the subject.

Would you like to be a part of the team?

Presently our team is very small. We are looking for students from all departments. We want both developers and content contributors. Any student who would like to be a part of our initiative can email us at jufet.github.io@gmail.com
