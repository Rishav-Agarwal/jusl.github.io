Vision

This is an educational website being created in hopes of helping students of Jadavpur University be better leaners . We want to be the best place to visit , if a student needs any help regarding the curriculum followed by departments in Jadavpur University . Presently , we are focused on Department of Information Technology , because of our familiarity with the courses . In future , if time and costs permit , we would surely expand to include other departments .

How do we plan to make our peers better learners ?

The objective is to host materials on all courses taught to the students in one place. Resources like lecture slides, assignment questions, previous year question papers , there solutions and class notes will be uploaded and will be open to all. We also plan to create study guides of various courses . It is important to study from the best place on Internet by the best people . We plan to accumulate all the videos sources and links which will surely increase the understanding of a course .


We hope , our efforts makes some difference in our peers lives :-)
